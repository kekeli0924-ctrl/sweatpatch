About Us

Our
Story

Mild dehydration is a silent performance killer. A small drop in hydration can show up as fatigue, irritability, slower thinking, and weaker focus—yet most people still rely on thirst, which often signals the problem too late.

NeuroHydro started as a student-built solution to a real daily pattern we kept seeing in ourselves and our peers: intense training, packed academic schedules, and long days—followed by unexplained dips in energy and mood.

We realized hydration wasn't just "physical." It was cognitive. So we built a wearable sweat patch that tracks electrolyte and pH trends in real time, then turns that data into clear, actionable guidance through a mobile app.

Our goal is simple: make hydration visible, measurable, and actionable—so students, athletes, and outdoor workers can protect their focus, stabilize mood, and perform at their best before dehydration becomes a problem.

Our Purpose

Mission

To empower every student, athlete, and outdoor worker to reach their peak mental and physical potential through science-backed, real-time hydration and electrolyte insights.

Our Values

Scientific Precision

We build with rigorous physiology and engineering at the core—prioritizing reliable sensing, signal quality, and meaningful interpretation of sweat data.

Proactive Prevention

We believe the best intervention happens early. NeuroHydro is designed to warn and guide before dehydration impacts performance.

Human-Centered Care

Data should feel supportive, not stressful—so our insights are designed to be encouraging, actionable, and easy to follow in real life.

Accessibility + Real-World Impact

We're building for everyday achievers—not just elite athletes—starting with students, athletes, and outdoor workers who need practical tools that fit daily life.

Long-Term Engagement

We care about habits, not hype—tracking patterns over time (hydration–mood–focus trends) and improving the product through feedback and iteration.
